Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Information We Collect

Hermes CornReform accesses your Google Workspace data solely to provide AI agent automation services. This includes:

- Google Docs: Read, create, and edit documents on your behalf
- Google Sheets: Access and modify spreadsheet data
- Google Calendar: View and manage calendar events
- Gmail: Read and send emails as directed by you

How We Use Information

Data is used exclusively for:

- Executing commands you authorize through the Hermes AI Agent
- Providing collaborative editing features
- Automating document workflows you specify

Data Storage

OAuth tokens are stored locally on your Raspberry Pi device. We do not maintain external databases of your Google data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. Your data is used solely for the services you request.

Security

OAuth 2.0 protocols ensure secure authentication. Tokens are encrypted and stored only on your local device.
